As you can see the water usage required to feed the animals in the animal agriculture industry are staggering. There is also a staggering amount of this water that is returned to the system as toxic animal waste, that then pollutes clean water sources

One of the saddest facts related to animal agriculture is that we have 700mn people on the earth starving for food everyday. Yet 70% off all the grain we grow, goes to feeding animals to get big really fast to meet profits. These un-natural growth habits then lead to making us as humans more un-healthy
